**Prevention Checklist**

**Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist (For Students in India)**

**1\. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone — even if they claim to be from the bank.**    
**2\. Remember: Entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.**    
**3\. Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unexpected payment requests.**    
**4\. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.**    
**5\. Always verify the identity of buyers/sellers before making online transactions.**    
**6\. Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) — avoid third-party or unknown apps.**    
**7\. Enable app lock and two-factor authentication for banking and payment apps.**    
**8\. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for unusual activity.**    
**9\. Do not share personal details like Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details on untrusted websites.**    
**10\. Use secure internet connections — avoid making payments on public Wi-Fi.**

**In case of fraud:**    
**\- Report immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in**    
**\- Call Helpline: 1930 (24x7) for quick action**
